# Design Context — Hiboo Baby Skincare

## Target Audience
Indonesian mothers (Moms), ages 22–38, digitally savvy, active on TikTok/Instagram/WhatsApp. Shop on Shopee, Tokopedia, TikTok Shop. Prioritize safety certifications (BPOM, Halal), natural ingredients, and peer recommendations. 73% shop via mobile.

## Use Cases
- Discover safe, certified baby skincare products for tropical climate
- Find solutions for eczema, ruam popok, biang keringat, gigitan serangga
- Compare products, read reviews, check certifications before purchasing
- Learn about baby skincare through educational blog content (SEO-driven)
- Buy via marketplace links (Shopee, TikTok Shop) — no on-site checkout

## Brand Personality & Tone
- **Warm & Caring** — Like a trusted friend who happens to be a pediatrician
- **Confident but Not Pushy** — State benefits clearly, never aggressive
- **Educational** — Teach moms about ingredients and tropical skin challenges
- **Japanese-Inspired Elegance** — Mochi Hada concept, refined and subtle
- **Trustworthy** — Certifications (BPOM, Halal, Derma-tested) always visible

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Soft, rounded, baby-safe** — 16–24px border-radius, generous padding, soft shadows
- **Color palette:** Sky Blue (#4AAFE0), Cream (#FFFCF8), Navy (#1B5F8A), Warm Cream (#FFF6ED)
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (headings) + Nunito (body/UI)
- **Never:** Purple, neon, dark red, black primary, sharp edges, aggressive contrast
- **Decorative:** Mochi/cloud blob shapes, hippo mascot as subtle accents
- **Tropical warmth:** Soft blue-to-cream gradients reflecting Indonesia's climate

## Differentiators
- #1 Baby Skincare on TikTok Indonesia (viral in 4 months, 7000% growth)
- Mochi Hada (mochi skin) concept — unique Japanese philosophy positioning
- Triple-certified: BPOM + Halal MUI + European Dermatologically Tested
- Formulated by Pediatricians & Dermatologists for tropical climate
- Brand Choice Award 2024 winner

## Tech Stack
- Static HTML/CSS/JS (converting to WordPress for blog CMS)
- No on-site ecommerce — links to Shopee/TikTok Shop
- Deployed on DigitalOcean (nginx, port 8080)
- SEO-optimized for Indonesian search (Bahasa content, JSON-LD schemas)

## Primary Language
Bahasa Indonesia. Japanese terms as brand vocabulary. English for scientific credibility.
