## Creative Principles

### The 2-Second Rule
Every ad must hook within 2 seconds. For Indonesian moms scrolling, the hook must be:
- A **visible problem** (red rash, crying baby, sleepless mom)
- A **surprising result** (perfect mochi skin, sleeping baby)
- A **bold claim** ("#1 di TikTok" or "50.000+ Moms sudah pakai")
- A **relatable scenario** ("Udah coba semua tapi ruam bayi nggak hilang?")

### Visual Hierarchy for Static Ads
1. **Hero image** — Product or before/after (largest element)
2. **Headline** — 1 line, big, bold (Nunito 700–800)
3. **Trust stack** — BPOM + Halal + Rating badges (small but always present)
4. **Price/Promo** — Crossed-out price + sale price + savings
5. **CTA button** — "Belanja Sekarang" in Hiboo Blue

### Color Rules for Ads
- Background: Hiboo Cream (#FFFCF8) or Baby Blue (#E8F4FB) — NEVER white
- Text: Charcoal (#1E2D3D) for readability
- Accent: Hiboo Sky Blue (#4AAFE0) for CTAs and highlights
- Price: Bold in Sky Blue or contrasting gold (#E8A838) for promos
- Never use red for prices (looks cheap/aggressive)

### TikTok Ads — Video Script Framework

**Hook (0–2 sec):** Problem or surprising statement
- "Bayi kamu sering merah-merah?"
- "Ternyata 65% bayi Indonesia punya masalah kulit ini..."
- "Ini skincare bayi #1 di TikTok"

**Problem (2–5 sec):** Elaborate on the pain point
- Show relatable scenario
- Quick stats or social proof

**Solution (5–12 sec):** Introduce product
- Show product clearly
- Highlight 2–3 key benefits
- Demonstrate texture/application

**Proof (12–18 sec):** Build trust
- Trust badges (BPOM, Halal)
- Star rating + review count
- Before/after or testimonial clip

**CTA (18–20 sec):** Drive action
- "Link di keranjang kuning!"
- "Klik sekarang, gratis ongkir!"
- Show price + promo if applicable

## Audience Targeting

### Core Audiences (Meta/TikTok)
- **New Moms:** Women 22–30, interests: pregnancy, newborn care, baby products
- **Experienced Moms:** Women 28–38, interests: parenting, toddler care, family
- **Eco-Conscious Moms:** Interests: organic, natural products, halal lifestyle
- **Problem Seekers:** Interests: eczema, baby rash, sensitive skin, dermatology

### Lookalike Audiences
- Based on: past purchasers, engaged Instagram followers, website visitors
- 1% lookalike for high-intent conversion campaigns
- 3–5% lookalike for awareness campaigns

### Retargeting
- Website visitors (7, 14, 30 day windows)
- Add-to-cart abandoners (priority — show promo code)
- Past purchasers (30–60 days — cross-sell and replenishment)
- Video viewers (50%+ view — show product-specific ad)

## Performance Benchmarks

### Target KPIs (Indonesia Baby Care category)
- **Meta CPM:** Rp 15K–30K
- **Meta CPC:** Rp 500–1,500
- **Meta CTR:** 1.5–3%
- **TikTok CPM:** Rp 10K–25K
- **TikTok Video View Rate:** 15–25%
- **Marketplace ROAS:** 5–8x minimum
- **Conversion Rate (website):** 2–4%
- **Conversion Rate (marketplace):** 4–8%
