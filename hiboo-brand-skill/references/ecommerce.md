### Image 1 (Hero/Thumbnail)
- Clean product shot on soft gradient background (Hiboo Baby Blue to Cream)
- BPOM + Halal badge overlaid in corner (small, not distracting)

- White/clean background behind product for Shopee/Tokopedia compliance

## Pricing & Promo Strategy

### Price Architecture
- **Trial Entry:** Rp 12K–35K (5g balm, 15ml serum) — loss-leader for first purchase
- **Core Range:** Rp 49K–89K — "affordable premium" sweet spot
- **Bundles:** 15–30% discount vs individual purchase — always show savings amount
- **Big Size:** Rp 89K–129K — best value per ml/gram

### Promo Mechanics That Work
1. **Voucher stacking:** Platform voucher + shop voucher + flash sale (perceived 40–60% off)
2. **Buy 1 Get 1:** Most effective for trial conversion (Serum + Free Zzz Cream)
3. **Bundle savings:** "Hemat Rp XX.000" — always show the absolute savings
4. **Free shipping:** "Gratis Ongkir se-Indonesia" — critical conversion driver
5. **Flash sale timing:** 9PM–11PM (moms' browsing time after kids sleep)

### Promo Codes
- Always uppercase, memorable: MOCHISKIN, HIBOOBABY, KULITMOCHI
- Include in announcement bar, product images, and checkout reminders
