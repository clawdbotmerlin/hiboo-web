## 1. All Purpose Baby Balm

**SKUs:** 5g (travel/trial), 15g (standard)
**BPOM:** Registered
**Halal:** MUI Certified
**Award:** Brand Choice Awards 2024 — Baby Balm Category

### Ingredients
Petrolatum, Cocos Nucifera (Coconut) Oil 10%, Synthetic Beeswax, Tocopheryl Acetate (Vitamin E), Bisabolol, BHT, Fragrance

### Key Claims
- Nourishing & Rash Relief
- Helps with: eczema, ruam popok (diaper rash), gigitan serangga (insect bites), bekas luka (scars), biang keringat (heat rash), kulit kering (dry skin)
- Suitable for newborn (0+ months)
- Can also be used by adults
- Alcohol-free, natural ingredients

### Usage Instructions
1. Bersihkan kulit bayi (Clean baby's skin)
2. Oleskan hiboo All Purpose Baby Balm pada area kulit yang bermasalah — kemerahan, gatal, atau kering (Apply to problem areas)
3. Dapat juga digunakan untuk orang dewasa (Can also be used by adults)
4. For areas with wet/oily rash: dry first with saline solution 0.9%, apply gauze, wait to dry, then apply balm

- **Starter Kit:** Baby Balm 5g + Mochi Serum 15ml + Zzz Cream 15g
- **Triple Mochi Skin:** All three core products in full size
- **Day & Night Kit:** Mochi Sunscreen + Mochi Body Serum
- **Buy 1 Get 1:** Mochi Serum + Free Zzz Cream (common promo format)
- **Travel Size Bundle:** 5g Balm + 15ml Serum + 15g Cream

## Pricing Psychology

- Trial/sachets at Rp 12K–35K to lower barrier to first purchase
- Core range Rp 49K–89K hits "affordable premium" sweet spot
- Bundles always show the "hemat" (savings) amount prominently
- Always use Rp notation (not IDR) for Indonesian audience
- Show crossed-out original price when running promos
