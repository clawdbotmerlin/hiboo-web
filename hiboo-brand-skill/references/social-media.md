### Content Pillars (aim for balanced mix)

1. **Educational (30%)** — Baby skincare tips, ingredient explainers, tropical climate skin challenges
   - Carousel format: 5–7 slides
   - Headline slide with hook question: "Kenapa kulit bayi sering merah di Indonesia?"
   - Use Hiboo blue + cream color palette
   - End slide: product recommendation + CTA

2. **Product Showcase (25%)** — New launches, hero product features, texture shots
   - Clean photography on brand-colored backgrounds
   - Show product in use (mom applying on baby)
   - Include size & price on image

3. **Testimonial/UGC (25%)** — Real mom reviews, before/after, reposted stories
   - Screenshot format with star rating overlay
   - Tag the original poster
   - "Terima kasih Moms @[handle]! 💙"

4. **Promo & Sales (15%)** — Flash sales, bundle deals, special events (Harbolnas, payday)
   - Countdown stickers in Stories
   - Clear price + savings amount
   - Swipe-up / Link in bio CTA

5. **Behind the Scenes (5%)** — Team, production, founder story
   - Humanize the brand
   - Show the care that goes into formulation

## TikTok Strategy (@hiboo.baby)

### Content Formats That Work

1. **Problem → Solution (highest performing)**
   - "Bayi kamu eczema parah? Coba ini..."
   - Show the problem (rash, dry skin) → apply product → show result
   - 15–30 seconds, hook in first 2 seconds

2. **Mom Testimonial / POV**
   - Real moms sharing their experience
   - Authentic, unpolished, relatable
   - "POV: akhirnya nemu cream yang cocok buat bayiku..."

3. **Product Demo / Texture**
   - Close-up of product texture, application
   - ASMR-style opening, swatching
   - Show how lightweight/fast-absorbing it is

4. **Before & After**
   - Day 1 vs Day 7 skin transformation
   - Must be genuine (never fake or exaggerated)
   - Include disclaimer if needed

5. **Educational Quick Tips**
   - "3 hal yang JANGAN dilakukan saat bayi ruam"
   - Numbered list format, fast-paced
   - End with product recommendation

### TikTok Live Shopping
- **Schedule:** Weekly, 8–10 PM (prime mom browsing time)
- **Host:** Bidan (midwife) or mom influencer
- **Format:** Education + demo + exclusive live-only bundles
- **Flash deals:** Drop discount codes during live for urgency
- **Engagement:** Answer questions in real-time, address specific skin concerns

### TikTok Shop Optimization
- Product cards linked in all videos
- "Keranjang Kuning" (yellow basket) always active
- Price shown directly on video overlay when relevant

## Influencer / KOL Strategy

### Ibu Ambassador Program
- **Tier 1: Nano (1K–5K followers)** — 300+ ambassadors, product gifting + commission
- **Tier 2: Micro (5K–50K followers)** — 100+ ambassadors, paid partnership + product
- **Tier 3: Macro (50K–500K followers)** — Select partnerships, campaign-based
- **Tier 4: Celebrity** — Jessica Iskandar & Hagia (existing relationship)

### Ambassador Content Guidelines
- Must use product for at least 7 days before posting
- "Honest review" format — not scripted
- Show real baby (with parental consent)
- Include product name, key benefit, and where to buy
- Tag @hiboo.baby + use #hiboomochiskin
- Disclose paid partnership: "Gifted by hiboo" or "Paid partnership"

### Content Approval Process
1. Ambassador submits draft content
2. Hiboo team reviews for brand alignment
3. Approve / request edits
4. Ambassador posts with proper tags and disclosure

## Community Management

### Response Guidelines
- Reply to every comment within 4 hours during business hours
- Tone: warm, helpful, uses emojis sparingly (💙 👶 ✨)
- For skin concern questions: provide general advice + recommend relevant product
- Never make medical diagnoses or guarantee cures
- For negative feedback: acknowledge concern, offer to DM for resolution
- For product questions: provide direct, clear answers with links

### WhatsApp Community
- Exclusive groups (max 50 members each)
- "Komunitas Ibu Cerdas" branding
- Weekly "Tanya Bidan" Q&A sessions
- Early access to flash sale vouchers
- Birthday milestone celebrations for babies

### Crisis Response
- Product complaints: respond within 2 hours, offer replacement
- Negative viral content: do not engage publicly in arguments, DM directly
- Medical concerns: always recommend consulting a pediatrician
- Never delete negative comments (unless spam/inappropriate)
